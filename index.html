Getting Started

How to Offset with CarbonKerma

Purchase and retire CKT credits in four simple steps. As a regulated platform, we require identity verification to ensure the integrity of every transaction.

1

Create Your CarbonKerma Account

Visit dashboard.carbonkerma.com and sign up. You can use the platform on both desktop and mobile — no app download required.

2

Complete KYC / KYB Verification

To comply with Anti-Money Laundering (AML) regulations and ensure the integrity of our carbon credit marketplace, all users must complete identity verification through our secure Argos KYC/KYB process. This is a one-time step that protects both you and the platform.

What You'll Need
Individuals (KYC): Government-issued photo ID (passport, driver's license, or national ID)
Businesses (KYB): Company registration documents, authorized representative ID, and proof of business address
3

Buy CKT Credits on the KermaExchange

Once verified, browse available CarbonKerma Tokens (CKT) on our KermaExchange marketplace. Each CKT represents exactly one tonne of CO₂ that has been physically captured and permanently stored underground through CCUS, verified under EPA-approved MRV protocols.

Your Purchase is Protected By
✓ Blockchain immutability — every transaction is permanently recorded
✓ KermaExchange Guarantee — credits replaced 1-for-1 if integrity is ever questioned
✓ Patent-pending Unique Carbon Tag (UCT) traceability
4

Retire CKT & Receive Your Offset Certificate

When you're ready to claim your offset, "burn" (retire) your CKT tokens. This permanently removes the tonnage from circulation on the blockchain — it can never be resold or double-counted. You'll receive an auditable Offset Certificate and a Unique Carbon Tag (UCT) proving the provenance of your offset.
